Words of Wis'Dam

International Student Guide Amsterdam 2021 / 2022

As students, we are constantly taking in all sorts of wisdom through literature, projects and (online) lectures to power through the lectures and turn into full-fledged professionals. Likewise, we gather wisdom from around the world to gain the most out of life outside of classes. With the Wis’Dam Tiles Generator, you can create your own wisdom tiles by choosing from a wide variety of Dutch wisdom, world wisdom, or your own wisdom.

Tiles of Wisdom are traditional Dutch tiles with proverbs or aphorisms inscribed on them, often in Delftware style, often used as decorative wall-hangings. They can typically be found in the toilet room and hold useful – and sometimes humorous – wisdom for the toilet goer to learn from. Hence the name ‘tegeltjeswijsheid’, or ‘tile wisdom’. The first known tiles of wisdom date back to the mid-16th century.
